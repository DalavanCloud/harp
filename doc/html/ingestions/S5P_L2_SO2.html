S5P_L2_SO2

Variables

The table below lists the variables that are present in the HARP product that results from an ingestion of S5P_L2_SO2 data.

field name type dimensions unit description
scanline_pixel_index int16 {time}   pixel index (0-based) within the scanline
datetime double {time} seconds since 2000-01-01 start time of the measurement
longitude float {time} degree_east longitude of the ground pixel center (WGS84)
latitude float {time} degree_north latitude of the ground pixel center (WGS84)
longitude_bounds float {time, 4} degree_east longitudes of the ground pixel corners (WGS84)
latitude_bounds float {time, 4} degree_north latitudes of the ground pixel corners (WGS84)
instrument_longitude float {time} degree_east longitude of the sub-instrument point (WGS84)
instrument_latitude float {time} degree_north latitude of the sub-instrument point (WGS84)
instrument_altitude float {time} m altitude of the instrument (WGS84)
solar_zenith_angle float {time} degree zenith angle of the Sun at the ground pixel location (WGS84)
solar_azimuth_angle float {time} degree azimuth angle of the Sun at the ground pixel location (WGS84), measured East-of-North
viewing_zenith_angle float {time} degree zenith angle of the instrument at the ground pixel location (WGS84)
viewing_azimuth_angle float {time} degree azimuth angle of the instrument at the ground pixel location (WGS84), measured East-of-North
SO2_column_number_density float {time} mol/cm^2 vertically integrated SO2 column density
SO2_column_number_density_avk float {time, vertical} cm averaging kernel for the vertically integrated SO2 column density
index int32 {time}   zero-based index of the sample within the source product
